# Project Insights & Conclusion

This project analyzes a simulated e-commerce marketplace using the Flipkart product dataset.  
The objective was to explore sales performance, product quality, and seller operations through interactive dashboards built using **Power BI**, supported by **Python and SQL Server** for data preparation and analysis.

The project was structured into three analytical dashboards:

- **Sales Dashboard** – Focused on revenue performance, units sold, and category-level sales trends.
- **Product Dashboard** – Analyzed product quality, ratings distribution, and brand performance.
- **Seller Dashboard** – Evaluated seller performance, delivery timelines, and stock availability.

---

# Key Insights

### Sales Insights
- Sales distribution varies significantly across product categories.
- A small group of products generates a large share of overall revenue.
- Units sold and revenue patterns highlight the most commercially successful products.

### Product Insights
- Product ratings across categories show relatively consistent averages.
- Certain brands consistently maintain strong product ratings.
- Highly rated products often receive large numbers of reviews, indicating strong customer engagement.

### Seller Insights
- Sellers contribute differently to overall marketplace activity in terms of units sold.
- Delivery times remain relatively consistent across sellers in the dataset.
- Stock availability across categories indicates balanced inventory distribution.
