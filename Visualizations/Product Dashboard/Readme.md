# Product Dashboard

The **Product Dashboard** focuses on analyzing **product quality, customer feedback, and brand performance** within the Flipkart product dataset.  
It provides insights into how products are rated, how brands perform, and which products receive the highest customer engagement.

---

# Dashboard KPIs

The top section of the dashboard contains **key summary metrics** that provide a quick overview of product performance.

### Average Product Rating
Displays the **overall average rating across all products** in the dataset.

### Total Reviews
Shows the **total number of customer reviews** recorded for all products.

### Average Product Score
Represents the **Average Product Score available** in the dataset.

### Total Brands
Indicates the **number of unique brands** present in the dataset.

### Total Categories
Indicates the **number of unique product categories** present in the dataset.

These KPIs help users quickly understand the **scale of the product catalog and overall customer sentiment**.

---

# Average Rating by Category

**Visual Type:** Bar Chart

This visualization shows the **average product rating across different product categories**.

### Purpose
- Identify which categories receive **higher customer satisfaction**.
- Compare customer ratings **across product categories**.

### Example Insights
- Some categories consistently receive **higher ratings**.
- Lower-rated categories may indicate **product quality issues or expectation gaps**.

---

# Average Rating by Brand

**Visual Type:** Bar Chart

This chart displays the **average rating for products grouped by brand**.

### Purpose
- Evaluate **brand performance based on customer ratings**.
- Identify brands that maintain **higher product quality and satisfaction levels**.

### Example Insights
- Brands with higher average ratings may indicate **stronger product quality**.
- Helps identify **brand performance trends** within the marketplace.

---

# Top Rated Products

**Visual Type:** Table

This table highlights products with the **highest ratings and review counts**.

### Fields Displayed
- **Product Name**
- **Sum of Ratings**
- **Sum of Review Count**

### Purpose
- Identify **highly rated products**.
- Highlight products that receive **strong customer engagement**.

### Example Insights
Products with both **high ratings and high review counts** typically indicate **high customer trust and product popularity**.

---

# Dashboard Objective

The **Product Dashboard** helps answer important analytical questions such as:

- Which **product categories receive the highest ratings**?
- Which **brands have the best customer satisfaction**?
- Which **products are most highly rated by customers**?

This dashboard complements the **Sales Dashboard** by focusing on **product quality and customer feedback**, enabling a more complete view of **marketplace performance**.
