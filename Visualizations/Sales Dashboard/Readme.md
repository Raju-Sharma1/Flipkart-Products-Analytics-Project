# Sales Dashboard – Flipkart Product Analytics

## Overview
The **Sales Dashboard** was developed to analyze and visualize overall sales performance from the Flipkart product listings dataset.  
It provides a high-level view of key sales metrics and helps identify trends in product performance, category revenue, and brand contribution.

The dashboard enables quick exploration of business insights by transforming analytical data into an easy-to-understand visual format.

## Key Business Questions Addressed

This dashboard focuses on answering key business questions such as:

1. **Which categories generate the highest revenue**

2. **Which products sell the most units**

3. **What is the overall sales and revenue performance**

4. **How sales are distributed across products and brands**

---

## Dashboard Components

### KPI Summary
Displays high-level metrics that summarize overall business performance:

- Total Revenue
- Total Units Sold
- Total Products
- Average Discount Percentage
- Average Customer Rating

---

### Revenue by Brand
A Clustered Column chart showing how total revenue is distributed across different brands, helping identify top-performing brands.

---

### Revenue by Category
A stacked bar chart illustrating revenue contribution by product category, highlighting categories that generate the most sales.

---

### Top Products Table
A table displaying top-performing products based on units sold and revenue, along with their associated brands.

---

### Units Sold vs Final Price
A scatter visualization used to explore the relationship between product price and total units sold.

---

## Purpose of the Dashboard
The Sales Dashboard serves as a **business performance overview tool**, allowing analysts and stakeholders to quickly understand:

- Sales trends
- Product demand
- Revenue distribution across categories and brands

It acts as the **first layer of business intelligence** in the Flipkart Product Analytics project, providing a foundation for deeper analysis such as pricing strategy and seller performance.
